Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID Mini 1
  • Flour 2 cups (200 gm)
  • Grated Paneer 600gms
  • Sooji/Rava 200gms
  • Baking Powder 1½ tsp
  • Baking Soda 1½ tsp
  • Oil For Frying
  • Water 2 litres
  • Sugar 1 kg
  • Coarsely Crushed Cardamom 6-8 nos

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup and remove from the fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round Gulab Jamuns. Fry in the oil at a very low flame till golden brown.
  4. Add the fried Gulab Jamuns to the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the Gulab Jamuns are in the sugar syrup, bring it to a boil and remove from the flame.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID 1/4 tin
  • Nestlé a+ Toned Milk 1/4 cup
  • Butter 150 g
  • Powdered Sugar 150 g
  • Eggs 3
  • Maida ( Flour) 125 g
  • Cocoa Powder 3-4 tbsp (25 g)
  • Baking Powder 1.5 tsp
  • Vanilla Essence 1 tsp
  • Cocoa Powder 4 tbsp
  • Butter 50 g

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 180° C. Lightly grease an 8-inch diameter baking tin and dust with flour.
  2. Sieve together maida, cocoa powder, and baking powder. Keep aside
  3. Soften butter in a bowl, add powdered sugar, and beat well to a creamy consistency. Add Vanilla essence and mix. Slowly beat in the eggs one by one, then fold in the sieved maida mixture and mix well. Add milk and mix to get a dropping consistency.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking tin and bake in the preheated oven for 40 -45 minutes or till a toothpick inserted in the centre of the cake comes out clean.
  5. Remove from oven, and cool for a while. Turn out over a wire rack or plate.
  6. For Fudge Icing: Mix Nestlé MILKMAID, cocoa powder, and butter and beat well. Microwave for 20 seconds and mix well.
  7. Spread over the cooled cake and leave to set. Slice and serve.

Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID Mini 1
  • Maida (All-purpose Flour) 2½ cup (250 gms)
  • Salt 1/2 tsp
  • Butter 100 gms
  • Cold Water 1/3 cup
  • Apples, peeled and sliced 4 cups
  • Brown sugar 2 tsp
  • Cinnamon Powder 1 tsp
  • Lime juice 1 tbsp
  • Butter 2 tbsp
  • Maida 1 tbsp

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 180⁰ C. Lightly grease an 8” loose bottom pie dish and dust with flour.
  2. In a large bowl, place maida with salt. Rub the butter into the flour with your fingertips until the mixture resembles bread crumbs. Add cold water little by little and gather the flour into a dough. Wrap in a cling film and keep in the refrigerator for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Take out the dough and place it on a floured surface, roll out the dough and cut into a circle 2” wider than the pie dish. Keep aside the extra dough.
  4. Place this dough carefully over the pie dish and press it into the pan, covering the sides. Trim the edges so it covers the rim of the dish.
  5. Prick with a fork at the bottom and on the sides. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes till the pie crust is lightly brown. Take it out of the oven.
  6. Now place apple slices in a bowl, sprinkle with brown sugar, cinnamon, and lime juice, and 1 tbsp maida. Mix well.
  7. Spread apples in the pie crust and pour Nestlé MILKMAID over the apples. Dot with half of the butter.
  8. Roll out the rest of the dough into ¼ thick sheet. Cut into strips. Place the strips, like an interwoven lattice, over the apple layer. Brush with the rest of the butter and bake in the hot oven for 25-30 minutes till the top turns golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven, cool, and carefully take out from the pie dish onto a serving plate. Slice and serve.

Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients

  • Nestlé MILKMAID Mini 3 Tbsp
  • Nestle A+ Milk ¾ Cup, chilled
  • Cocoa Powder 1 ½ Tbsp (8g)
  • Warm Water 2 Tbsp
  • Ice Cubes 2-3 Crushed

Recipe

  1. Mix Cocoa Powder with warm water till no lumps.
  2. Add the cocoa mixture and rest of the ingredients in a mixer jar and blend till smooth.
  3. Transfer in a tall glass.
  4. Serve chilled.
